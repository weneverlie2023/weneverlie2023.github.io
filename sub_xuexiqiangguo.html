XUEXIQIANGGUO APP | 学习强国

Learning Power is a theoretical learning platform run by the propaganda department of the CPC Central Committee, featuring Xi Jinping's remarks and ideas, integrates news aggregation, instant messaging, and social networking functions.The pun of the term "learning to strengthen the country" can be interpreted as "learning to make the country a stronger country," which is based on Mao Zedong's inscription "Study hard and improve every day." The app has been heavily promoted by government agencies and party members have been encouraged to download it, according to Chinese state media.With more than 100 million active users as of October 2019, it is now said to be the most downloaded app on Apple's China App Store, overtaking social media apps such as WeChat and TikTok.
