Chinese dream | 中国梦

Since becoming general secretary of the Communist Party of China in 2012, Xi has come up with a concept known as the "China Dream."The core slogan of strengthening ideological education of the Party and state puts forward the goal of "realizing the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation".In the language of the PRC's propaganda department, the Chinese Dream includes several statements: The China Dream emphasizes the development of China into an economically prosperous country.This means maintaining rapid growth in global economic competition, raising the level of the national economy, while maintaining political stability in the country. "National rejuvenation": realizing the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation is the core content of the Chinese dream.This includes the inheritance and promotion of China's excellent traditional culture, the maintenance of national unity, and the enhancement of national pride and identity. "People's happiness": The Chinese dream focuses on the well-being of all people, including improving the living standards of the people, ensuring social equity and justice, improving education and medical care.At the same time, the Chinese Dream emphasizes that all the people participate in the process of national development and national revitalization and give full play to the main role of the people. "Harmonious Society": The Chinese dream advocates building a harmonious socialist society, which means all-round development in economic, political, cultural and social fields, reducing the gap between the rich and the poor and improving people's well-being. "International status enhancement": The Chinese dream emphasizes the enhancement of China's position and influence in international affairs.This includes active participation in global governance, promoting the construction of a community of human destiny, maintaining world peace and development, and promoting global economic, political and cultural multipolarity.
